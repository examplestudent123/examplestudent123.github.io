Mr. Corry's Explorations of Character

Defining Character

Character is one’s ability to consistently make correct choices. Your character is demonstraited by how well you use your time. An individual’s character may also be defined by the lack or presence of specific qualities or virtues such as honesty, integrity, humility, courage, etc. The development of such virtues enhance one's character.

Quote about Character

"Most people say that it is the intellect which makes a great scientist. They are wrong: it is character." - Albert Einstein
